Ultiorganizer is a scoring system for Ultimate tournaments. Scorekeeping can be done live or after the match. In addition to game results, Ultiorganizer provides spirit scores, schedules, team and player statistics and much more.

  • Game schedule - Tournament schedule
    • Game History - If teams have played against each other earlier, then this link is shown.
    • iCalendar - The iCalendar link on the bottom of the page allows you to import game schedule into other services like Google Calendar.
    • Printing - You can print schedules or get PDF versions by following the links at the bottom of the page.
    • Game play - When scoresheets have been entered into the system, you can see the links next to games.
  • Teams - Teams playing in tournament
    • Seedings - You can list teams by division, pool, seedings or final standing.
    • Team card - By clicking on team names you can see their team card.
    • Club card - For club teams you can find the club card here as well.
    • Player card - By clicking on player names you can see player cards with statistics and other information about the player.
    • Roster - You can see team rosters with player statistics.
    • Scoreboard - Here you can find your team's internal scoreboard.
    • Games - Lists past and coming matches in the tournament.
  • Divisions and pools
    • Event - By clicking on the event name you see the final standings.
    • Division - By clicking the division name you see overall stats and scoreboard.
    • Pool - By clicking on pool names you see the pool's stats and scoreboard.
  • Statistics - If activated, you can see statistics from previous tournaments
    • Events - All tournaments with statistics available.
    • Players - All player cards.
    • Teams - All team cards.
    • Clubs - All club cards.
    • Countries - All country cards.
    • All time - Hall of Fame.
